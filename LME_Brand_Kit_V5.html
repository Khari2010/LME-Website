01

WHO WE ARE

LME is a five-piece live band formed in 2023 out of Birmingham, blending Dancehall, RnB, Afrobeats, Pop, Gospel, Disco, and Soca into high-energy live performances and unforgettable events.

We’ve shared stages with Stormzy, Tems, Headie One, Sugarbabes, Alex Isley, Fireboy DML, and Gambimi, and performed at Silverstone F1 and the Qatar World Cup.

Think The Compozers’ live credibility meets Going To Granny’s event energy, with the cultural cool of NSG and WSTRN, and the genre-fluid versatility that makes every set feel fresh.

Brand Personality

ENERGY HYPE PARTY CREW QUALITY

Our Audience

Ages 20–35. RnB, dancehall, bashment, afrobeats heads. They want proper musicianship and they want to move.

For bookings: Weddings, brand launches, content openings, artist after-parties. The client who wants their night talked about.

What Makes Us Different

The genre range, the energy, and the arrangements. LME brings a broader palette than anyone in this lane, Birmingham roots, and a party-first mentality that turns every set into an event.

06

BRAND VOICE

Slang-forward but never sloppy. Fun and familiar to the culture, readable for someone booking a wedding band.

Write like you’re texting your mates about a sick night — but your manager might read it too.

Instagram
“Last night was DIFFERENT. If you know, you know. Bookings open for summer — link in bio.”
Casual, hype, direct.
Event Promo
“LME presents: WORLD CUP PARTY. Live sets. Good people. No dead vibes. July 18th.”
Punchy. Short sentences. Urgency.
Booking Response
“Thanks for reaching out! We do weddings, launches, and after-parties — and we bring the energy guaranteed. Let’s set up a call.”
Professional but warm.
YouTube
“LME live at [venue]. Full set, no edits, just vibes. Subscribe — more sets dropping soon.”
Minimal. Music does the work.

Content Direction

Fun but well-produced. High-quality musicianship captured with energy. Band-focused — the members are the hero of every frame.

DO

Band-focused shots: members performing, instruments in action, on-stage energy.

DO

Well-lit, colour-graded footage. Show the musicianship visually.

DO

BTS that still looks good — soundcheck, rehearsal. Candid but intentional.

DON’T

Lead with crowd footage. The band is always the hero.

DON’T

Post shaky, dark, or low-quality content.

DON’T

Use generic stock imagery or templates.
